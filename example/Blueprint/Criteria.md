# Criteria

## Budget

Self-funded by two founders working part-time. Spending is limited to hosting, the
email provider, and store fees, on the order of low hundreds of dollars a month.
Runway is indefinite as long as costs stay below early subscription revenue; there
is no outside money and no burn target beyond "do not lose money on infrastructure."

## Objective and scope

The objective for this cycle is a **production MVP**: a small team can sign in, save
articles, read them, and annotate together, reliably enough to pay for. That ceiling
rules out anything that does not serve capture, recall, or shared annotation. No
mobile app, no public sharing, no AI summarization yet — each is a different product
surface and would pull scope past what two people can support.

## Timeline

Private beta with five friendly teams by end of Q3. Paid launch gated on retention,
not a date: the team will not open billing until beta teams come back unprompted in
week four. The browser extension must clear each store's review before launch, so
extension work leads the rest by several weeks.
